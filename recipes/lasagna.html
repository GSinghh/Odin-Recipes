Lasagna Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet italian sauasage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two gloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and Seasonings: This lasagna reciple is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: use store bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzeralla, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasanga as you cut into it.

Make it yourself! Here are the steps!

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna accoring to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
