Chicken Taco Recipes

Look at these delicous tacos!

Let's show you how to make them!

Here are the ingredients you will need

For the chicken:

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin.
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika.
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.
  • Juice of two limes.
  • One tablespoon olive oil.

For the toppings:

  • 1/3 cup finely chopped red or sweet onion
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped cilantro
  • 1 finely chopped jalapeno
  • Juice of one lime
  • Salt and Pepper, for taste
  • 1 lime, cut into 8 pieces.

Instructions:

  1. In a large zipper-topped plastic baggie or in shallow dish, add the chicken, all of the spices, lime juice, and oil. Close the bag and use your hands to mix everything together.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium to medium-high heat. When the skillet is hot, add a tablespoon of oil to the pan and then add the chicken. Cook for 12 to 14 minutes, turning once, until the chicken is cooked through.
  3. While the chicken cooks, stir together the chicken taco topping by placing the chopped onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and the juice of one lime in a small bowl. Add salt and pepper, and stir to combine.
  4. Place two tortillas (still stacked together) on the skillet and cook until the underside starts to char. Flip over and cook the other side.
  5. Chop the rested chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  6. To serve, place ⅛ of the chopped chicken in each set of tortillas (still two stacked together) and top with ⅛ of your onion mixture. Serve each taco with a wedge of lime to squeeze over the taco just before serving.
